Upbringing

Kyle Lowry was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania in 1986. He grew up in a tough neighbourhood where he faced many challenges, including poverty and violence. Despite these obstacles, Lowry remained focused on basketball and worked hard to improve his skills. He attended Cardinal Dougherty High School, where he played on the basketball team and earned a reputation as a tough, physical player. After high school, Lowry played college basketball at Villanova University, where he helped lead the team to the Elite Eight in the NCAA tournament. Lowry declared for the 2006 NBA draft and was selected 24th overall in the first round by the Memphis Grizzlies. Lowry’s upbringing in a tough environment helped shape him into the resilient and determined player he is today.
